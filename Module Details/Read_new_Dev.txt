Overview
WorkNest is a modern Laravel HRM application built for companies, HR teams, managers, and employees. It helps manage the complete employee lifecycle from hiring to retirement, including employee records, attendance, leave, payroll, loans, recruitment, onboarding, performance, assets, helpdesk, reports, and self-service features.

Key Highlights
Complete HRM Suite
Employee management, departments, designations, branches, teams, documents, HR records, and employee profiles.

Payroll, Loan & Provident Fund
Salary structures, payroll run, payslips, loans, advance salary, bonuses, reimbursements, PF, tax, and statutory settings.

Attendance, Shifts & Leave
Check-in/out, attendance corrections, shifts, rosters, attendance policies, holidays, leave requests, approvals, balances, and encashments.

Employee Self-Service
Employees can view attendance, apply for leave, download payslips, submit requests, view assets, trainings, documents, and more.

Modern Admin Experience
Livewire-powered admin panel, global search, quick create, notifications, audit logs, demo mode, and reusable UI components.
